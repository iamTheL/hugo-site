About Me

Padmanabhan here, I browse stackoverflow.com for my living. I work as full time developer at Pramati Technologies I design and develop interesting solutions for various domains. Most of my experience in building my solutions revolved around the Retail industry.

To mention a few:

  1. Warehouse solution
  2. Omnichannel and Store Design

Having graduated as an Embedded Systems Engineer automating STUFFS through code still fascinates me. As life has got its own way of presenting itself, I became an Software Engineer. Still my fascination for SBC and microcontrollers hasn’t faded and I plan to write about it more in this blog. I will be writing about system design problems and solutions to address them based on my experience.

After my 9-5 job I promise not to bore the crap out of myself. I try to think(over-think) a lot and translate them into words and love to hear how the voices in my head sound to others. All such posts will be tagged as rabbit-hole for you to dive into it.
